THE SIGNIFICANCE OF MEHENDI

In Indian Weddings

Mehendi, or henna, is more than just a decorative art form in Indian weddings. It symbolizes beauty, joy, and spiritual awakening. Traditionally, the application of Mehendi is believed to bring good fortune and happiness to the bride's married life. The darker the color of the Mehendi, the deeper the love between the couple, according to popular belief. This makes the ritual not just a visual treat but also a meaningful tradition embedded with deep cultural significance.
