Base Brillance -

A Flawless Canvas!

First things first, let’s talk about that base. Want your makeup to last through all the festivities? Slather on some primer; it’s like giving your face a smooth ride. And here’s a secret: Mix a tiny drop of facial oil with your foundation for that natural, dewy glow. A flawless base, achieved effortlessly!

WING IT, SIS!

Eyes are where the magic happens. Ran out of eyeliner? No problemo! Grab a thin brush and turn your eyeshadow into an instant liner. Wing it out for that extra oomph—you’re rocking a Bollywood look now.

Brow Game Strong -

HAIRSPRAY

Hacks!

Brows are the frame for your face, and we’re going bold. No brow gel? No sweat! Spritz some hairspray on a clean mascara wand and tame those brows. They’ll stay in place all day, defying gravity and turning heads.

Blush Bliss -

Rosy Cheeks, No Stress!

Dreaming of that blushing bride look? Use your lipstick as a cream blush. Dab a bit on your cheeks, blend it in, and there you go—instant rosy radiance! Easy-peasy multitasking for that bridal glow.

LIPSTICK

That Lasts -

Eat, Drink, Slay!

Are you worried about your lipstick surviving the feast? After applying your lipstick, press a tissue over your lips and dust on some translucent powder. It’s like setting your makeup in stone—eat, drink, and still slay.

Hair Tailes -

volume & curls Galore

Now, onto those gorgeous tresses. Want extra volume? Flip your hair upside down while blow-drying. And for heatless curls, try the classic rag-rolling technique. Healthy, happy hair—that’s the goal!

Accessory Adventures -

Pins, Clips, & Passa Love!

Accessories can level up your hairstyle game. Running low on hairpins? Use bobby pins in an X shape for a secure hold. And that stunning passa? Keep it in place with tiny hidden clips—dance the night away without a worry!

Emergency Essentials -

Bridal Survival Kit!

Stuff happens, right? Pack a mini bridal survival kit with bobby pins, blotting paper, a mini hairspray, and your lipstick for quick touch-ups. You’re now armed to tackle any last-minute surprises.
