So, you’ve got the love bug, and now you’re knee-deep in wedding planning chaos, especially when it comes to managing that guest list. Fear not! We’re here to spill the beans on some super-friendly, absolutely free online tools that’ll turn your guest list game into a piece of wedding cake.

Google Sheets

YOUR DIGITAL BFF

Let’s start with a classic: Google Sheets. It’s like having a magical planner that never loses a page. Create a sheet for your guest list—names, RSVPs, special notes, the whole shebang. Bonus: You can update it while sipping chai or lounging on the couch.

Perfectly
Wed

YOUR DIGITAL BFF

Enter PerfectlyWed, the superhero of wedding planning. It’s like a magical wand that turns chaos into order. Use it to craft your guest list, track RSVPs, and even send out digital invitations. It’s your virtual wedding planner, and it won’t cost you a paisa!

f you’re all about saving the trees and keeping things chill, meet Evite. Create cool digital invitations, and let Evite handle the RSVP tracking. It’s like hosting a virtual party before the real one—eco-friendly and fuss-free.

Trello

WHERE VISUALS MEET
ORGANIZATION

For the visual thinkers in the room, Trello is your artsy friend. Imagine your guest list as a beautiful board. Move guests through stages like ‘Invited,’ ‘RSVP Received,’ and ‘Gifts Received”—a visual treat for your organized soul.

In the wild world of wedding planning, these free online tools are your fairy godmothers, turning your guest list worries into confetti-filled celebrations. Whether you’re a spreadsheet ninja, a tech enthusiast, or just want things pretty and visual, there’s a tool for you. So, go ahead, dive into the world of easy-breezy wedding planning, and let these tools make your journey as smooth as butter on naan.
